Courses Offered

Govt Polytechnic Siddipet offers specialized diploma programs designed to build strong foundations in modern engineering.

CSE

Computer Science Engineering

The CSE department focuses on the basics of computer programming, networking, and software development. Students learn to design, build, and test software applications and systems.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Programming Languages (C, Java, Python)
  • Web Development & Database Management
  • Computer Networks & Cyber Security

ECE

Electronics & Communication Engineering

The ECE department deals with electronic devices, circuits, and communication equipment. It provides a blend of hardware and software knowledge for modern technology.

Key Topics Covered:
  • Digital Electronics & Microprocessors
  • Analog & Digital Communication Systems
  • Embedded Systems & IoT
